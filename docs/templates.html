Template Usage Instructions

1. Choose Your Format

Select the template format that best fits your workflow and documentation requirements.

2. Fill in the Details

Replace all placeholder text (shown in brackets) with your specific project information and requirements.

3. List Permissions

Document all Microsoft Graph permissions your application requires, organized by risk level (Critical, High, Medium, Low).

4. Describe Data Flows

Detail how data moves between your application and Microsoft Graph, including direction and data types.

5. Address Security

Include security measures, compliance requirements, and risk mitigation strategies for your integration.

6. Upload & Analyze

Upload your completed project plan to the Risk Analysis Engine for automated risk assessment.
